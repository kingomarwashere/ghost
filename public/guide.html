What is Ghost?

Ghost is a free, no-sign-up navigation app for Australian drivers — a Waze-style map with live crowdsourced police sightings, fixed speed & red-light camera alerts, real-time turn-by-turn voice navigation, traffic detection and time-based school-zone warnings. It runs in your browser and installs to your home screen.
